/ MINELOADER STUDIOS

We are a full-pipeline game development and art production outsourcing partner. For over 20 years we have been solely dedicated to console game development, growing to a team of more than 500 full-time professionals across Shanghai, Tianjin, and Dalian.

In 2025 we became part of Winking Studios Group, expanding our global reach while remaining exactly who we are. The work, the team, and the standard we hold ourselves to have not changed.
