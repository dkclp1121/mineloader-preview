Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2024

This Cookie Policy ("Policy") is issued by Mineloader Studios Limited ("Mineloader", "we", "us", or "our"). This Policy applies to all websites operated or controlled by Mineloader.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, such as enabling websites to function properly, tracking user activity, and remembering preferences.

Cookies can be classified by their origin, function, and lifespan:

  • First-party cookies — Placed directly by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party cookies — Placed by a website other than the one you are visiting. We do not control the collection or use of data by third parties.
  • Necessary cookies — Essential for the technical operation of a website (e.g., navigation and core functionality).
  • Performance cookies — Collect aggregated data on website performance, such as visitor numbers and error rates.
  • Functionality cookies — Enhance usability by remembering user preferences such as language and region.
  • Targeting / advertising cookies — Enable personalized advertising based on browsing behavior.
  • Session cookies — Temporary cookies erased when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies — Remain on your device until deleted manually or based on a specified duration.

Purpose of Using Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Distinguish between visitors and maintain session continuity;
  • Enhance the functionality and usability of our websites;
  • Tailor our website content to your needs and preferences;
  • Analyze website usage and compile anonymous, aggregate statistics.

We do not use collected information to create individual visitor profiles.

  • Functionality Cookies — Remember your preferences and record acceptance of our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies by adjusting your browser settings:

  • Internet Explorer / Edge
  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Opera

Disabling cookies may impact your experience. If you delete cookies, you may need to re-accept our Cookie Policy on your next visit.
