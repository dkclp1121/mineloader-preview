Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2024

Mineloader Studios Limited ("Mineloader", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who interact with our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read this Policy carefully before submitting any personal information to us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information in connection with our website and business operations, including recruitment:

  • Name, gender, and age;
  • Educational background and current occupation or major;
  • Contact details including phone number, email address, and messaging app handles;
  • Work experience, skills, and qualifications;
  • Portfolio links and work samples;
  • Job preferences, desired position, and expected compensation.

This information may be collected through online application forms, résumés, cover letters, interviews, references, or other related documents.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Assess your suitability for open positions at Mineloader Studios;
  • Contact you regarding your application or interview arrangements;
  • Match you with appropriate roles based on your stated preferences and qualifications;
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Sharing and Disclosure

Your information will only be used for Mineloader Studios purposes and accessed only by team members responsible for relevant operations. We may share your information with authorized third-party service providers solely for legitimate business purposes.

We will not disclose your personal information to external parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or supplement your personal information;
  • Request deletion of your personal information;
  • Withdraw consent or restrict the scope of processing;
  • Exercise any other rights granted under applicable data protection laws.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by applicable law.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and collect analytics data. For details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
